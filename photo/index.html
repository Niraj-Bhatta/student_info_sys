Programs Offered - Pokhara University

Bachelor of Computer Engineering

Focuses on computing, programming, and problem-solving techniques.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Core Subjects: Programming, Data Structures, Networking, AI, and more.
  • Career Prospects: Software Engineer, Network Administrator, AI Specialist, etc.

Bachelor of Information Technology

Emphasizes on information systems, software, and digital communication.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Core Subjects: Web Technology, Database Management, Cyber Security, and more.
  • Career Prospects: IT Analyst, Web Developer, Cyber Security Expert, etc.

Bachelor of Electronics and Communication Engineering

Covers electronics, communication systems, and embedded systems.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Core Subjects: Digital Signal Processing, Embedded Systems, Communication Systems, etc.
  • Career Prospects: Communication Engineer, Embedded Systems Developer, etc.

Bachelor of Civil Engineering

Focuses on planning, designing, and supervising construction projects.

  • Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)
  • Core Subjects: Structural Analysis, Hydraulics, Geotechnical Engineering, and more.
  • Career Prospects: Civil Engineer, Structural Analyst, Construction Manager, etc.
